Meet Our Development Team

We are passionate students from Hong Kong, driven by innovation and the desire to make technology accessible to everyone. Our journey began in the classrooms of Po Leung Kuk No.1 W.H. Cheung College, where we learned that great ideas can come from anywhere.

Po Leung Kuk No.1 W.H. Cheung College

Our educational foundation comes from Po Leung Kuk No.1 W.H. Cheung College in Hong Kong, where we've been inspired to use technology for social good. The college's emphasis on innovation, community service, and academic excellence has shaped our approach to developing solutions that truly make a difference in people's lives.

Our Story

From classroom inspiration to real-world impact

Academic Foundation

Our journey started in computer science and AI courses at Po Leung Kuk No.1 W.H. Cheung College, where we learned the fundamentals of programming, machine learning, and user experience design.

Identifying the Need

Through research and community outreach, we discovered the significant challenges faced by hearing-impaired individuals in learning speech. This sparked our mission to create accessible solutions.

Innovation & Impact

Combining our technical skills with deep empathy, we developed Sound by Sound Slowly - an AI-powered platform that makes speech learning accessible, engaging, and effective for everyone.

Our Values

The principles that guide our development and mission

Accessibility First

We believe technology should be inclusive and accessible to everyone, regardless of their abilities or background.

Innovation

We constantly push the boundaries of what's possible, combining cutting-edge AI with human-centered design.

User-Centric

Every feature we develop is designed with our users in mind, ensuring the best possible learning experience.

Community Impact

Our goal is to create positive change in our community and beyond, one user at a time.

Looking Forward

As we continue our studies and grow as developers, we remain committed to our mission of making speech learning accessible through technology. We're proud to represent the innovative spirit of Hong Kong students and Po Leung Kuk No.1 W.H. Cheung College in the global tech community.

Our journey has just begun, and we're excited to continue pushing the boundaries of what's possible in AI-powered education and accessibility technology.
